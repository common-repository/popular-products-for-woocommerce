Shwon your most popular/viewed post with view count
== Description ==
This is a simple widget plugin to show wc popular products of your WordPress website based on views.You can easily control this plugin from dashboard.

***Featured***

*** shown your wc popular products

*** Shown the number of product as you wish

*** You can show/hide count of visit.

*** You can show/hide rating of product.

How to use:-
very easy to use,after active plugin  just go to appearance->widget. then add "Popular Products" anywhere your widget area or use [ppfw_propular_products] as a shortcode any where. it's enough, Let's enjoy it.


== Installation ==

**The easy way.**

Go to your WordPress Dashboard. Navigate to Plugins > Add New and then search for "Popular Products for WooCommerce". Click on Install and then Activate the Plugin.

That's it, "Popular Products for WooCommerce is now activated on your site!

**The hard way..**

Download "Popular Products for WooCommerce" and then extract the zip file and just drop the contents in the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation and then activate the Plugin from Plugins page.
